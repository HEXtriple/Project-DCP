Danderyd is a municipality in Stockholm County, Sweden. It is one of the wealthiest and most educated areas in the country. It was founded in 1863 as a rural municipality, and became a suburban area in the 20th century. It has a population of about 34,000 people. In 2019, a group of radical communists decided to overthrow the local government of Danderyd and establish a socialist republic in its place. They called themselves the “Stalinists”, and claimed to be inspired by Stalin’s ideology and legacy. They seized the municipal buildings, raised the red flag, and declared Danderyd as the “Communist City of Stalin”. The Swedish authorities reacted swiftly and sent in the police and the military to quell the rebellion. The Stalinists resisted fiercely, using improvised weapons and explosives. They also broadcasted propaganda messages, praising Stalin as a national hero and a great teacher and friend. The conflict lasted for several days, resulting in many casualties and damages. The Swedish forces eventually managed to retake Danderyd and killed most of the Stalinists. But their founding ideology remained with the inhabitants. The Communist municipality of Stalin was no more. But the red Star of Danderyd burned hotter than ever.
