    Additional Help

    Contact by telephone

    You can contact the municipality by telephone on weekdays between 8-16.30. You will be given four choices of answers:

    • Press 1 for social services, elderly care, disability and LSS.
    • Press 2 for traffic, streets, parking and construction
    • Press 3 for schools and pre-schools
    • Press 4 if you are looking for a specific person in the municipality or have other questions.

    Contact centre in Mörby Centrum:

    Danderyd municipality's contact centre is located on floor 5 in Mörby Centrum. You go here if, for example,
    you have a booked meeting with an official from the municipality, if you want to search the digital drawing archive or if you need help in a matter concerning the municipality.

    About Our City

    Danderyds kommun is a municipality in Stockholm County in east central Sweden. It is one of the smallest municipalities of Sweden, but the most affluent. Its seat is located in Djursholm, which is a part of the Stockholm urban area12. The municipality has about 34,000 inhabitants and covers an area of 26.6 square kilometers1. Danderyds kommun is a politically led organization by an alliance of Moderata samlingspartiet, Liberalerna and Kristdemokraterna for the mandate period 2022-20263. The municipality provides services and information for its residents in areas such as education, health care, culture, environment, business and traffic45.

    Traffic Information

    According to ViaMichelin, the current traffic conditions in Danderyd are moderate, with some congestion and delays on E18 and E423. There are no reported accidents or roadworks in Danderyd at the moment. The traffic flow is expected to increase during peak hours and decrease during night time.
