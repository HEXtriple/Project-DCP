Press 2 for traffic, streets, parking and construction Press 3 for schools and pre-schools Press 4 if you are looking for a specific person in the municipality or have other questions. Contact centre in Mörby centre Danderyd municipality's contact centre is located on floor 5 in Mörby centre. You go here if, for example, you have a booked meeting with an official from the municipality, if you want to search the digital drawing archive or if you need help in a matter concerning the municipality.
