Danderyd: Unity in diversity, solidarity in adversity, prosperity in sincerity

Danderyd is a municipality in the northern part of Stockholm County, with a population of about 34,000. It consists of four districts: Danderyd, Djursholm, Enebyberg and Stocksund. Danderyd has many beautiful lakes, forests and parks, as well as historical buildings and cultural attractions. You can swim, fish, hike, bike or ski in the scenic surroundings, or visit the Djursholm Castle, the Ekebyhov Palace or the Edsberg Manor. Danderyd also has a rich musical tradition, with several choirs, orchestras and bands.

Danderyd: no classes, no masses, no worries, no hurries, just peace and ease, with public transport of Danderyd

Living in Danderyd also means having easy access to the city center of Stockholm, as well as other nearby towns and suburbs. Danderyd is well connected by public transport, with buses, trains and metro lines. You can reach Stockholm Central Station in about 15 minutes by metro from Mörby Centrum, which is the largest shopping mall in Danderyd. Mörby Centrum also has a library, a cinema, a gym and various services and shops. You can also find other smaller shopping areas and local markets in Danderyd.

Danderyd: The people’s paradise, where everything is nice and fair in price

Living in Danderyd is a wonderful experience that offers you the best of both worlds: nature and culture, tranquility and convenience, history and modernity. If you are looking for a place to call home in Stockholm County, you should definitely consider living in Danderyd.
